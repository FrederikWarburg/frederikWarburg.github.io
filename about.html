Frederik Warburg

I'm Head of AI at Teton.ai. We are building the next generation of healthcare technology using computer vision and deep learning to help nurses and patients in hospitals and nursery homes. We work at the forefront of research in 3D reconstruction, human mesh recovery, action recognition, and visual-language models. Please reach out if you are interested in collaborations, research internship or job opportunities.
