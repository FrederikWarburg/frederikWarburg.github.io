About Me

I am studying Mathematical Modelling and Computing and have chosen to specialise within machine learning and computer vision. I am part of the honours program at DTU, which is an elite education that offers a more challenging course of study, individual tutoring and ambitious research affiliation. My general areas of interest are machine learning, computer vision and autonomous robots. My research focuses on uncertainty-aware deep learning for autonomous driving.

I received my BSc in Mathematics and Technology Engineering in 2018 and began my master's studies in 2017. The second year of my MSc studies was spent as an exchange student at the Department of Electrical Engineering, University of California, Berkeley. Furthermore, I visited Marc Pollefeys' Visual Computing Group at ETH Zurich in 2019 and Javier Civera Computer Vision Group at the University of Zaragoza in 2018. I have industry experience from Beep Analytics (Denmark), Mapillary (Sweden), AutoDesk (Denmark) and IBM (Denmark).
